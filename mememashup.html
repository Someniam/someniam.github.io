Meme Mashup

This art piece is one I made in Photoshop. I combined two famous things into one singular mashup, in this case "Star Wars" and the "Bliss" wallpaper used in Windows XP. I have made a picture depicting the AT-AT walkers attacking the hill while the Shaak (sheep-like creatures from Episode II) are wandering about in the plains. I have enjoyed Star Wars for a long time and it is the reason I decided to create this photoshop mashup. The end result resembles a scene from the movies.
