I made a game that involves sports. I decided to make it so that in level one, you have a yo-yo with a string where you upgrade at level two to a kickball and upgrade again at level three to a golf ball. The difficulty of the game changes because of the change in hitbox size. At 50 points you win the game and earn a trophy!
